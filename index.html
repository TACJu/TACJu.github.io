Ju He

Ju is a research scientist at Amazon Frontier AI & Robotics (FAR). Previously, he was a research scientist at ByteDance. He received his Ph.D. degree from Computer Science department at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan L. Yuille.

Before that, he obtained his B.S. degree from Computer Science department at Peking University in 2020.
