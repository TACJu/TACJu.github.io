Ju He

I am a second-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Johns Hopkins University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan L. Yuille.

Before that, I received the B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University. I have also spent great time at ByteDance, SenseTime, and Momenta. I will spend my next summer at Meta as a Research Scientist Intern.

I'm exploring various learning-based computer vision problems. Currently, I am interested in image segmentation and egocentric vision.
